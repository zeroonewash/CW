Meet Craft22 Artisans

Welcome to Craft22, where creativity knows no bounds! Nestled in the vibrant heart of Brooklyn, NY, our team of skilled artisans is on a mission to sprinkle a little magic into your life with our handmade crafts. From custom jewelry that makes you feel like royalty to home decor that transforms your space into a sanctuary, we’ve got it all!

Our passion for artisanal crafts is matched only by our love for coffee and sarcasm. We believe that every piece we create tells a story, and we’re here to help you find the perfect chapter for your home or wardrobe. So, whether you’re looking for a statement piece or a subtle touch, we’ve got you covered!

Join us on this wild ride of creativity and craftsmanship. We promise you’ll leave with something that not only looks fabulous but also has a soul. Because let’s face it, mass-produced junk just doesn’t cut it!
